Man Liu   刘曼

I am currently currently a lecturer with the School of Artificial Intelligence, Anhui University (AHU), Hefei, China. Prior to that, I received Ph.D. degrees from the School of Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Jiaotong University (BJTU) in 2025, under the supervision of Prof. Huihui Bai and Prof. Yao Zhao. From 2023 to 2024, I was a Research Scholar of NExT++ Lab, under the supervision of Prof. Tat-Seng Chua.

Research Interests

My research interests cover a range of computer vision and deep learning, such as fine-grained visual recognition, zero-shot learning, generative modeling and learning, and visual-and-language learning. I have authored or co-authored academic papers in top-tier international conferences and journals, including CVPR, AAAI, TPAMI, TIP, TMM, TCSVT etc. I'm open to any kind of collaboration.
